Privacy Policy

Effective: July 22, 2026

This policy applies to all applications published by MLM Games under the developer account on Google Play.

Data Collection

None of our applications collect, store, or transmit any personal data. We do not use analytics SDKs, crash reporting services, advertising frameworks, or any third-party libraries that collect user information.

Permissions

Some apps may request device permissions (e.g., storage, camera, location) solely for the app's core functionality. This data is processed exclusively on-device and is never transmitted off the device.

Third-Party Services

Our apps do not integrate any third-party services that collect user data. If an app requires network access (e.g., a BitTorrent client or Matrix chat client), that connection is user-initiated and no data is sent to us.

Children's Privacy

Our apps do not knowingly collect any personally identifiable information from children under 13.

Changes

If this policy changes, we will update this page.
